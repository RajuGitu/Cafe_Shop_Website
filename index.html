About Us

Cafe Indian Touch in Ghatkopar East, established in 2015, is renowned not only for its good ambience and delicious food but also for its unique birthday decorations. The cafe offers personalized decor options that transform the space into a festive haven. From colorful balloon arches and elegant floral arrangements to themed table settings and custom banners, every detail is thoughtfully designed to create a memorable celebration. Whether you prefer a traditional Indian aesthetic or a modern twist, Cafe Indian Touch ensures your birthday celebration is unique and unforgettable.
